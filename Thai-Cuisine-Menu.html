Best Selling Thai Foods
Sample Image Name Description
Tom Yum Goong Tom yum goong is a hot and sour Thai soup that's made with shrimp, mushrooms, and shrimp stock. The soup's name comes from the Thai words "tom yam", which mean "boiling" or "hot" and "spicy and sour".
Bibimbap Bibimbap is a dish that combines rice, vegetables, meat, sesame oil, and gochujang in a single bowl to be eaten with a spoon and chopsticks. It is now one of the most well-known names in Korean cuisine. Bibimbap offers a truly unique package of flavor and nutrition in a single meal.
Gaeng Daeng Gaeng Daeng, also known as Thai red curry, is a famous Thai dish. It's a blend of red curry paste, coconut milk, vegetables, and protein. The dish is known for its bold and fiery flavors.
Khao Pad Khao pad, also known as Thai fried rice, is a dish of stir-fried rice. The name translates to "stir-fried rice".
Som Tum Som tum is a well-known spicy salad from Thailand that has its origins in Laos. The name comes from the Thai words som (sour) and tum (pounding). The dish is made by using a mortar and pestle to crush and mix the ingredients.
