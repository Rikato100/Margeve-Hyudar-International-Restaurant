About Us

Margeve Hyudar's International Cuisine Restaurant was established on October 04, 2022, under the leadership of Mr. Hyacinth Hyun and with the help of his advocates, Mr. Darry Malignos, Ms. Mary Connie Sentro, Ms Gennilyn Margarita, and Ms Vera Narehistro. The restaurant is located at #0203, Block 9, Lot 2, Fort Bonifacio, Taguig City, Metro Manila, Philippines.

The restaurant's aim is communicated in its motto, “Discover the globe on your plate. Margeve Hyudar, where flavors sync and resonate." This means not only do we aim to give you high quality food but also the best dining experience that revolves around the world.

A relatively new restaurant, Margeve Hyudar strives to surpass other top food chains by consistently delivering excellence in high-quality food and service. We take pride in our commitment to providing a unique culinary experience, blending various cultural cuisines such as Japanese, Italian, Filipino, and more.

At Margeve Hyudar, we believe that dining is not just about the food; it's an immersive experience. Our restaurant is meticulously designed with a cozy ambiance, offering a perfect setting for gatherings, celebrations, or a quiet dinner for two.

We take pride in our dedicated team of highly-qualified chefs, each a master of their own culture's foods. Our chefs bring diverse culinary expertise, ensuring that every dish is a celebration of authentic flavors.

Discover the joy of global flavors at Margeve Hyudar's International Cuisine Restaurant, where every visit is a journey around the world on your plate!

Vision, Mission, and Core Values

Vision

Our vision at Margeve Hyudar's International Cuisine Restaurant is to be more than just a dining establishment; we aspire to be an immersive culinary destination that transports our guests into a world of gastronomic wonders. Through meticulous craftsmanship and a passionate pursuit of culinary artistry, we aim to redefine the dining experience. We envision creating a global culinary haven that transcends boundaries, where each dish tells a story and every bite is a journey.

Mission

At Margeve Hyudar's International Cuisine Restaurant, our mission is to curate an extraordinary dining journey that goes beyond the ordinary. We are dedicated to providing an oasis of exquisite flavors, where each meal is a symphony of tastes, textures, and aromas. Our commitment extends to sourcing the finest ingredients from around the world, ensuring that every dish is a testament to authenticity and quality. Through a blend of innovation and tradition, we seek to captivate the palates of our patrons, offering a unique and memorable experience. Additionally, we aim to foster a sense of cultural appreciation by introducing our guests to the richness of international cuisines. Our restaurant serves as a melting pot of global flavors, inviting individuals to savor the diversity that defines the culinary world.

Core Values

1. Passion for Excellence: We are committed to delivering culinary excellence in every dish, ensuring a memorable and satisfying experience for our customers.

2. Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate the diversity of international cuisines and embrace an inclusive environment that welcomes people from all walks of life.

3. Customer-Centric Approach: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to exceed their expectations and provide unparalleled service.

4. Innovation and Creativity: We continuously explore new culinary ideas, techniques, and flavors, pushing the boundaries of traditional cuisine to create innovative and exciting dishes.

5. Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices, sourcing high-quality ingredients responsibly, and minimizing our environmental impact.

6. Team Collaboration: We foster a collaborative and respectful work environment, valuing the contributions of each team member towards our collective success.

7. Respect for All: No matter the ethnicity, appearance, gender, color, and status in life. We value most of all is respect for everyone, we believe that we all are equal and thus all deserve equal respect. This builds trust not only to customers but to our employees as well.
