Best Selling American Foods
Sample Image Name Description
Barbecue Ribs A dish of pork or beef ribs that are cooked with indirect heat and smoke or direct heat from a grill. Before cooking, the meat is often marinated or rubbed with seasonings to tenderize and flavor it. Barbecue ribs are typically served with barbecue sauce and eaten by hand.
Blueberry Cobbler Blueberry cobbler is a warm dessert with a blueberry base and a cake-like topping. The fruit bubbles into the topping as it bakes, creating three layers: a tart blueberry bottom, a dumpling-like center, and a crisp crust.
Delmonico’s Steak Delmonico steak is a thick, flat cut of beef from the cow's back. It's also known as a chuck eye steak or chuck filet steak. Delmonico steaks are tender and great for grilling. They have enough fat to produce a moist and tender dish, but the meat is a little tougher than cuts from the rib.
Pecan Pie Pecan pie is an old-fashioned pie made with pecans folded into a batter made of butter, sugar and eggs, that bakes into a delicious caramel, nutty pie filling. The filling is often made with corn syrup, but other variations include white or brown sugar, cane syrup, molasses, maple syrup, or honey.
Milkshakes Typically made with milk, ice cream, and whatever flavor or sweetener strikes your fancy, milkshakes have been delighting Americans since the 1880s. They were originally served up as medicine, but folks eventually figured out that milkshakes were more of a treat than a tonic.
