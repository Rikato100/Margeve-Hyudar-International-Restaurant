Best Selling Chinese Foods
Sample Image Name Description
Peking Roast Duck Peking duck is a dish from Beijing that has been prepared since the Imperial era. The meat is characterized by its thin, crispy skin, with authentic versions of the dish serving mostly the skin and little meat, sliced in front of the diners by the cook.
General Tso's Cauliflower with Sriracha Rice General Tso's Cauliflower with Sriracha Rice: This recipe features extra crispy fried cauliflower and a homemade sweet, sour, and spicy sauce. The cauliflower can be served over Sriracha rice.
Wonton Soup Wonton soup is a Chinese soup made with seasoned chicken broth and filled wontons. Wontons are dumplings filled with pork and/or shrimp, vegetables, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
Spring Rolls Spring rolls are a savory Chinese appetizer made from a thin, crispy wrapper filled with minced vegetables, seafood, poultry, or meat. The wrapper is made from dough or rice paper and can be baked, deep fried, or steamed.
Pearl Milk Tea Pearl Milk Tea most commonly consists of tea accompanied by chewy tapioca balls ("boba" or "pearls"). It has many varieties and flavors, but the two most popular varieties are pearl black milk tea and pearl green milk tea ("pearl" for the tapioca balls at the bottom).
