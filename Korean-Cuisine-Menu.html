Best Selling Korean Foods
Sample Image Name Description
Sundubu Gyeran Sundubu Gyeran Guk is made with extra soft tofu and eggs, this soup is the ultimate high protein breakfast. It’s warm and comforting. Because of its mild taste and smooth texture, the soup is also excellent for the ill and young children.
Bulgogi Deopbap Bulgogi deopbap is a Korean rice bowl dish made with marinated, thinly sliced beef and steamed rice. The word "deopbap" translates to "covered rice".
Gamja-jeon Pancakes Gamja-jeon or potato pancakes is a variety of jeon, or Korean style pancake, made by pan-frying finely grated potato on a frying pan with any type of vegetable oil until golden brown.
Korean Spinach Korean spinach, commonly known as "sigeumchi" in Korean cuisine, is a versatile leafy green vegetable with tender leaves and a mild, slightly sweet flavor. It is often used in various Korean dishes, both cooked and raw.
Kimchi Kimchi is a traditional Korean dish made from salted and fermented vegetables. It's a staple of Korean cuisine and is often served as a side dish with meals. Kimchi can also be used in soups and stews.
