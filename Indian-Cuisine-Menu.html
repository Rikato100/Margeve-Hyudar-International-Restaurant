Best Selling Indian Foods
Sample Image Name Description
Chicken Tikka Masala Chicken Tikka Masala is a dish of roasted marinated chicken chunks in a spiced sauce. The sauce is usually creamy and orange-colored. The dish is made up of two components: chicken tikka and the masala, which is the gravy or sauce in which the tikkas are simmered.
Pani Puri Pani puri, also known as golgappa or puchka, is a popular Indian street food. It's made by filling a small, hollow, deep-fried puri with a mixture of potatoes, chickpeas, and spiced water (pani). You can also add other fillings like chutneys, chaat masala, onions, and sev.
Samosa A samosa is a savory pastry that is popular in South Asia and the Middle East. Samosas can be baked or fried and come in many different shapes, including triangular, cone, or half-moon. The pastry is made of maida and has a flaky, crisp outer layer. The filling can vary widely, from potato with ginger and garlic to cauliflower, spiced lamb mince, minced fish, and chicken.
Masala Chaas Masala Chaas is a popular Indian yogurt drink. This refreshing drink is lightly spiced and is the perfect way to cool off during summers.
Gulab Jamun Gulab Jamun is an Indian dessert of fried dough balls that are soaked in a sweet, sticky sugar syrup. As per tradition, the syrup has a delicate rose flavour: Gulab means 'rose water' and jamun refers to a berry of a similar size and colour.
